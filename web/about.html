Fink

Monitoring big data is not easy. Given the input rate, it is hopeless and insane to want to display and look for everything. Instead, Fink focuses on the big picture via summary statistics sent by the different services. However individual alerts are not lost! They are organised and stored in large sinks, with fast access to enable post-processing.
